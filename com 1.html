Traffic Optimization Approaches Comparison

Performance analysis of different for traffic optimization over time

Baseline (General Traffic)

Standard traffic flow without any intelligent optimization systems. Serves as the control for comparing the effectiveness of other approaches.

Multi-Agent System (MAS)

Distributed autonomous agents that cooperate to optimize traffic flow. Each agent can monitor and respond to local traffic conditions.

Swarm Intelligence(CI)

Computational intelligence approach using neural networks to predict and optimize traffic patterns based on historical and real-time data.

Neural Network (SI)

Decentralized, self-organized system inspired by collective behavior of social insects. Uses simple rules to achieve complex optimization.
